About Me:

Dr. Priya Bhave Chittawar is one of the best and most trusted female gynecologists, IVF Specialist in Bhopal currently working as a Reproductive Medicine Specialist in Bansal Hospital, Bhopal. She achieved her MBBS degree from Gandhi Medical College and later went on to pursue M.D. (Obstetrics and Gynecology) from the same institute. She also holds a D.N.B. degree (Obstetrics and Gynecology) from National Board of Examination, New Delhi. Dr. Priya has expertise in Reproductive medicine and minimally invasive reproductive surgery. Reproductive medicine is a subspecialty that treats hormonal problems in both men and women, including subfertility. It uses assisted reproductive technologies like in vitro fertilization (IVF), intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI), egg donation, and a variety of reproductive tract surgeries like laparoscopy and hysteroscopy.
